    HOW TO PLAY MUSIC LEAGUE

    Share your favorite music

    A league is made up of rounds, and each round has a theme. When the round starts, you can submit a song that fits the theme.

    Discover new artists

    When everyone has submitted, we'll send you a playlist to listen to. After listening, you can vote and share comments.

    Compete to win the league

    When the votes are in, we'll shwo you the results and comments. The points you earn each round will help you in your quest to win the entire league!
